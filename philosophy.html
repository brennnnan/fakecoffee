Our Beginnings

Our story starts with a cold late night in Nepal where a few friends were trying to finish the preparations that had to be made for the next day's adventures. They realized that their needs were not being met by the starbucks of the world and they wanted to start a coffee shop that would provide all the best coffee while catering to adventurers, readers, and thinkers like them.
